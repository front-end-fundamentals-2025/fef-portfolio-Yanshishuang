A little game about a little owl coming home

Start-Play

In the heart of a dense forest, an adventurous little owl begins its journey back home. As night falls, the owl soars gracefully through the skies, but the journey isn't as simple as it seems. The owl needs your help! You see, as the owl flies down toward its nest, gravity is pulling it faster and faster. To ensure a safe and gentle landing, you must press the Spacebar to slow down its descent. The owl’s wings flap in response, but the more you press the spacebar, the slower the owl will fall. The challenge is to find the right balance — press too much and the owl might hover in mid-air, but don’t press enough and it will crash! Can you help the owl land softly in its cozy home without falling too fast? Test your reflexes and timing to see if you can guide the owl to safety!

You need to tap the space bar to control the falling speed of the little owl. If it is too fast, it will not successfully land on its home and the game will fail.
